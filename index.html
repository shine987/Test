About JFT Exam

The JFT (Japanese Language Test) is designed to measure the Japanese language proficiency of non-native speakers. These practice exams will help you prepare for the actual test by simulating the exam environment and question types. Regular practice with these tests will improve your reading, listening, and comprehension skills.(Credit to the original owners of the exam data resources.)
